Data Visualisation using Plotly

Note: IMDb data on 1st January 2020.

Rating of Each Episode


The bar graph shows the user ratings (weighted average) of the Start-Up drama by episode. There are a total of 16 episodes in the drama. The x-axis shows the episode of the drama while the y-axis shows the rating. Rating one means bad whereas, ten means excellent. Based on the graph, the weighted average rating shows declining trend by episode. Overall, the first episode has the highest weighted average rating while the final episode has the lowest rating with weighted average rating of 9.0 and 6.2 respectively.

Rating vs Total Votes


The histogram graph shows the user ratings of the Start-Up drama. The x-axis shows the number of votes while y-axis shows the rating from 1 to 10. Rating one means bad and ten means excellent. A total of 2,429 IMDb users has given a weighted average vote of 8.2/10 for this drama. Overall, there were 992 IMDb users that rated the drama as excellent, while 217 of them voted as bad drama. The majority of the users find that this drama is an enjoyable to watch.

Rating by Demographic


The bar graph shows the user ratings of the Start-Up drama based on age and gender category. The x-axis shows different age categories starting from all age category, under 18 years old, 18 to 29 years old, 30 to 44 years old and 45 years old and above. The x-axis also breaks down into three genders category which are both genders, male and female. The y-axis shows the rating from 1 to 10 where one means bad and ten means excellent.

From all age categories, more female voted that the drama is quite interesting with a weighted average rating of 8.2 than the male, just slightly lower by 0.1. Under 18 years old category and age 18 to 29 years old, male viewers feels that this drama is enjoyable with a weighted average rating of 9.0 and 8.2 respectively while female viewers has 8.5 and 8.1 weighted average rating for this drama.

For viewers age 30 to 44 years old and above 45 years old, female viewers have rated the drama with 8.2 and 8.8 weighted average rating respectively. The male viewers rated 8.1 and 7.8 respectively. Overall, female viewers aged 45 years old and above and male viewers under 18 years old has the highest weighted average rating for this drama with 8.8 and 9.0 weighted average rating respectively.

Overview Analysis

As an overview of the drama, the WordCloud and tri-gram shows a very high level summary of what the viewers are expecting from the drama. Some keywords or similar words were repeating indicating the main essence of the drama such as the second male lead and second lead syndrome, which appeared twice in this drama meaning that the second male lead is quite popular among the viewers.

Other words which would give a rough idea would be want hjp happy and hjp happy ending, which could be that the viewers would want hjp to be happy or the drama would be having a happy ending. In the WordCloud, love triangle, first love, and let go are also strong key indicators.
